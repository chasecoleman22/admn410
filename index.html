About Us

History

Durham House of Pets was established in 2003 by Ann Spaulding.Originally, Durham House of Pets was created to help the growing popoulation of stray animals around the Durham, New Hamshire area. Duraham House of Pets became known as "DHOP," and the place to go for adopting and putting up for adoption. Slowly over the years, Ann started carrying more and more products to cater to the majority of the pets coming through her store. Now in the present, DHOP has a large array of food choices, supplies, and toys, along with adoption center and kenel service. She has turned a small animal shelter into a thriving store and care center.


Why Use Us

Durham House of Pets is far from your typical pet shop. It has small town feel and service, so you can be reassured that your special one is being well taken care of.
